How to Play

🎡

Spin the Wheel!

Tap to spin and land on one of 8 trivia categories. Each spin is a new adventure!

Answer Questions

Waffles fetches a question just for you — powered by AI, so every game is unique!

Crown Challenge!

Land on the special space? Challenge your opponent and steal one of their paw stamps!

Waffles picks a wacky surprise question from ANY category. Anything goes!

Win the Crown!

First player to collect all 8 paw stamps earns The Golden Crown and victory pets from Waffles!
